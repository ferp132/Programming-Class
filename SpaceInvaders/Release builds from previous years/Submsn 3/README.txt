Welcome to this remake of the classic Space Invaders game.

CONTROLS:

Movement - Left and right arrows
Fire     - Space bar


GOAL:

Defeat wave after wave of alien invaders by shooting them out of the sky.
Avoid invader fire or you will lose a life.  You can gain an extra life
for every 10 levels complete.


SCORING:

Skull invader - 10
Toaster invader - 20
Conehead invader - 30
Mystery ship - 50, 100, 150 or 300
